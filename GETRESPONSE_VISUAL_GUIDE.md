# GetResponse Visual Click-by-Click Guide

## 🎯 EXACTLY Where to Click for Each Email Type

---

## PART 1: PLAIN TEXT EMAILS (Welcome Sequence)

### **Step 1: Access Autoresponders**

**What you see on screen:**
- Top navigation menu with: Dashboard, Contacts, Messages, etc.

**What to click:**
1. Click **"Messages"** in the top menu
2. From the dropdown, click **"Autoresponders"**

---

### **Step 2: Create New Autoresponder**

**What you see on screen:**
- Blue button that says "Create autoresponder"

**What to click:**
1. Click the **blue "Create autoresponder"** button

---

### **Step 3: Choose Autoresponder Type**

**What you see on screen:**
- Two options appear:
  - **"Autoresponder cycle"** (LEFT option)
  - **"Time Travel"** (RIGHT option)

**What to click:**
1. Click **"Autoresponder cycle"** (the LEFT one)
2. Click **"Next"** button at bottom

---

### **Step 4: Select Your List**

**What you see on screen:**
- Dropdown menu showing your lists

**What to click:**
1. Click the dropdown
2. Select **"Faith Funnels AI - Buyers"** (or your buyer list)
3. Click **"Next"** button at bottom

---

### **Step 5: Add First Email**

**What you see on screen:**
- Button says **"Add message"**

**What to click:**
1. Click **"Add message"** button

---

### **Step 6: Choose PLAIN TEXT Editor** ⭐ **IMPORTANT**

**What you see on screen:**
- You'll see THREE options:
  1. **"Create from scratch"** (LEFT - this is what you want!)
  2. **"Use template"** (MIDDLE)
  3. **"Use AI"** (RIGHT)

**What to click:**
1. Click **"Create from scratch"** (LEFT option)

---

### **Step 7: Choose Plain Text Format** ⭐ **CRITICAL STEP**

**What you see on screen:**
- Two big tiles:
  - **"Plain text message"** (LEFT tile) ← **CLICK THIS ONE**
  - **"HTML message"** (RIGHT tile)

**What to click:**
1. Click **"Plain text message"** (LEFT tile)

**Visual cue:** The plain text option usually shows a simple text icon, while HTML shows a design/layout icon.

---

### **Step 8: Enter Email Details**

**What you see on screen:**
- Form with fields:
  - Message name
  - Send on day
  - Subject
  - From field
  - Reply-to
  - Message content (big text box)

**What to fill in:**
1. **Message name:** "Welcome Email #1"
2. **Send on day:** 0 (sends immediately)
3. **Subject:** Copy from guide: `🙏 Welcome to Faith Funnels AI - Your Account is Ready!`
4. **From field:** Your name (already set)
5. **Reply-to:** support@faithfunnelsai.com
6. **Message content:** 
   - Click in the big text box
   - Paste the entire email from GETRESPONSE_SETUP_GUIDE.md
   - Replace `{{FNAME}}` if you want (GetResponse auto-replaces this)

---

### **Step 9: Save Your Email**

**What you see on screen:**
- Blue button at bottom right says **"Next"** or **"Save and publish"**

**What to click:**
1. Click **"Next"** button
2. Review screen appears
3. Click **"Save and publish"**

**✅ Done!** Your first plain text email is set up!

---

### **Step 10: Repeat for Emails #2-5**

**For each additional email:**
1. Click **"Add message"** again
2. Repeat steps 6-9
3. Change "Send on day" to: 1, 3, 5, 7 (for emails 2, 3, 4, 5)

---

## PART 2: HTML EMAILS (Launch Broadcasts)

### **Step 1: Access Newsletters**

**What you see on screen:**
- Top navigation menu

**What to click:**
1. Click **"Messages"** in top menu
2. From dropdown, click **"Newsletters"**

---

### **Step 2: Create New Newsletter**

**What you see on screen:**
- Blue button says **"Create newsletter"**

**What to click:**
1. Click **"Create newsletter"** button

---

### **Step 3: Choose Your List**

**What you see on screen:**
- Dropdown showing your lists

**What to click:**
1. Select your list (your main prospect list for launch emails)
2. Click **"Next"** button

---

### **Step 4: Choose Design Method** ⭐ **IMPORTANT**

**What you see on screen:**
- Three options appear:
  1. **"AI email generator"** (LEFT)
  2. **"Email creator"** (MIDDLE) ← **CLICK THIS ONE**
  3. **"Import HTML"** (RIGHT)

**What to click:**
1. Click **"Email creator"** (MIDDLE option)

---

### **Step 5: Browse Templates** ⭐ **KEY STEP**

**What you see on screen:**
- Left sidebar shows categories:
  - Blank templates
  - Popular
  - Promotional
  - Newsletter
  - Seasonal
  - E-commerce
  - etc.

**What to click:**
1. In left sidebar, click **"Promotional"** or **"Newsletter"**
2. Scroll through templates on the right

---

### **Step 6: Select the RIGHT Template** ⭐ **CRITICAL**

**What you're looking for:**
- Simple, single-column layout
- Minimal images
- Clear button/CTA area
- NOT multi-column
- NOT image-heavy

**Recommended templates to look for:**
- **"Basic"** 
- **"Simple"**
- **"One Column"**
- **"Newsletter Basic"**
- **"Promotion Simple"**

**Visual Guide - GOOD templates:**
```
GOOD ✅
┌─────────────┐
│   Header    │
│   Image     │
│   Text      │
│   Text      │
│  [Button]   │
└─────────────┘
Simple, clean, single column
```

**Visual Guide - BAD templates:**
```
BAD ❌
┌─────┬─────┐
│ Img │ Img │  ← Multiple columns
├─────┼─────┤
│ Img │ Img │  ← Too many images
└─────┴─────┘
Cluttered, multi-column
```

**What to click:**
1. Click on a **simple, single-column template**
2. Template preview appears
3. Click **"Select"** button on the template

---

### **Step 7: Customize the Template**

**What you see on screen:**
- Drag-and-drop email editor
- Left sidebar with elements
- Main editing area in center
- Your selected template is loaded

**What to do:**

**A. Edit the Header Text:**
1. Click on the headline text in the template
2. Delete placeholder text
3. Type your email headline (e.g., "🔥 LIVE NOW - Faith Funnels AI")

**B. Edit Body Text:**
1. Click on body text sections
2. Delete placeholder content
3. Paste your email copy from the guide

**C. Edit the Button:**
1. Click on the button element
2. Look for button settings (usually a sidebar appears)
3. Change button text to: "Get Started Now" or your CTA
4. Change button link to: `https://faithfunnelsai.com`
5. Optional: Change button color to match your brand

**D. Remove Extra Elements:**
1. If template has extra images/sections you don't need
2. Click on the element
3. Look for trash/delete icon
4. Click to remove

---

### **Step 8: Preview Your Email**

**What you see on screen:**
- Top right corner has buttons

**What to click:**
1. Click **"Preview"** button (eye icon)
2. Check how it looks on desktop and mobile
3. Make sure all text is readable
4. Verify button link works

---

### **Step 9: Save and Schedule**

**What you see on screen:**
- Top right has **"Next"** button

**What to click:**
1. Click **"Next"** button
2. Enter subject line (copy from guide)
3. Set **"From"** name and email
4. Click **"Next"** again
5. Choose **"Schedule for later"**
6. Set date and time (e.g., Launch day at 9 AM)
7. Click **"Schedule"** or **"Send now"**

**✅ Done!** Your HTML newsletter is scheduled!

## 🎨 TEMPLATE SELECTION CHEAT SHEET

### **In the Template Gallery, Look For:**

**✅ GOOD Template Names:**
- "Basic"
- "Simple Newsletter"
- "One Column"
- "Clean Promotion"
- "Newsletter Essential"
- "Product Launch Basic"

**❌ AVOID Template Names:**
- "Magazine"
- "Grid Layout"
- "Multi-Column"
- "Photo Showcase"
- "Image Gallery"

**✅ GOOD Visual Indicators:**
- Single vertical column
- 1-2 images max
- Clear button at bottom
- Lots of white space
- Simple header

**❌ BAD Visual Indicators:**
- Multiple columns side-by-side
- 5+ images
- Complex layouts
- Busy backgrounds
- Tiny text

---

## 📱 MOBILE PREVIEW CHECK

**Before finalizing ANY email:**

1. Click **"Preview"** button (top right)
2. Click **"Mobile"** tab
3. Check that:
   - Text is readable
   - Button is tappable
   - Images aren't too large
   - Nothing is cut off

**If it looks good on mobile, it will work!**

---

## 🔄 WORKFLOW SUMMARY

### **For Plain Text (Autoresponder):**
```
Messages → Autoresponders → Create autoresponder → 
Cycle → Select list → Add message → Create from scratch → 
Plain text message → Fill in content → Save & publish
```

### **For HTML (Newsletter):**
```
Messages → Newsletters → Create newsletter → Select list → 
Email creator → Promotional/Newsletter category → 
Select simple template → Edit content → Preview → 
Schedule/Send
```

## 💡 PRO TIPS

1. **Save Templates:** Once you customize a template you like, save it as "My Template" for reuse
2. **Test First:** Always send a test email to yourself before scheduling
3. **Check Spam Score:** GetResponse shows spam score - keep it under 5
4. **Use Preview Text:** Add preview text (first 40 characters people see in inbox)
