## STEP 5: Get Your Button Codes (10 minutes)

For each page, Warrior Plus generates unique buy button code:

1. On each Offer Page card, click **"Get Code"**
2. Choose **W+ Button** (or Pixel if using custom buttons)
3. **Copy the entire code** (HTML with tracking)
4. **Update your exported HTML files**:
   - Open each exported HTML file (main.html, oto1.html, etc.)
   - Find the CTA button section with the placeholder link
   - Replace the entire `<a>` tag with the Warrior Plus button code
   - Save the file
5. Also copy **"No Thanks" / Decline link codes** for pages with downsells
6. Upload the updated HTML files back to Warrior Plus

**Before:**
```html
<a href="https://warriorplus.com/o2/buy/your-product-id" class="cta-button">
  Get Faith Funnels AI Now
</a>
```

**After:**
```html
[Paste the exact button code from Warrior Plus here - it will include tracking]
```

## STEP 6: Validate Your Buttons (5 minutes)

**Critical step - make sure buttons work!**

1. On each Offer Page, click the **"!"** (exclamation icon) next to "Get Code"
2. Warrior Plus scans your sales page:
   - ✅ **Green checkmark** = Button detected correctly
   - ⚠️ **Warning/Error** = Fix the issue
3. Fix any errors and re-validate
4. **All pages must show green checkmarks before activating**

## STEP 7: Set Up Email Integration (Optional - 10 minutes)

Automatically add buyers to your email list:

1. Go to **Vendor → Integrations**
2. Connect your autoresponder:
   - GetResponse
   - AWeber
   - MailChimp
   - ConvertKit
3. In your Offer settings, enable **"Add to List"** for each product
4. Map to specific lists (e.g., "Faith Funnels Buyers")

---

## STEP 8: Activate Your Offer (Submit for Review)

1. Review your entire funnel one final time
2. Check the **Offer Map** at the bottom - shows funnel flow visually
3. Click **"Settings"** (top right of Offer Setup)
4. Click **"Activate Offer"**
5. Your offer goes into **Warrior Plus review queue**
6. **Approval time**: 24-72 hours typically

**What they review:**
- Product quality
- Sales page compliance
- Button codes working correctly
- No prohibited content

---

## STEP 9: Recruit Affiliates (After Approval)

Once approved:

1. Go to **Vendor → Offers → [Your Offer]**
2. Click **"Affiliate Link"** to get your recruitment URL
3. Share this link:
   - Email your existing list
   - Post in affiliate Facebook groups
   - Reach out to JV partners directly
4. Affiliates request approval in their dashboard
5. You **approve/deny** in **Vendor Dashboard → Affiliates**

**To attract top affiliates:**
- Create promotional materials (email swipes, banners, graphics)
- Offer review copies to proven affiliates
- Set up an affiliate contest with cash prizes
- Target 75-100+ EPC (Earnings Per Click)
